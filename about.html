Building India's Clean Energy Future, One Roof at a Time

Silisun Technology Private Limited is a Madurai-based renewable energy company on a mission to power homes, industries and institutions today — and manufacture the solar technology of tomorrow.

Our Story

From Installer to Innovator

Silisun Technology was founded in Madurai, Tamil Nadu with a clear purpose: make clean, reliable solar energy accessible to every home, business, farm, and institution across the region. We started by focusing on what matters most to our customers — honest consultation, quality components, and installations that simply work.

Today, we deliver rooftop and ground-mounted solar systems across residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural, educational, and government sectors. Tomorrow, our roadmap takes us further — into solar assembly and manufacturing, contributing directly to India's energy independence.

Growth Journey

Our Roadmap: Installation to Manufacturing

A clear, phased path toward becoming a vertically integrated solar manufacturing company.

2026 – 2028

Solar Installation Services

Establishing Silisun as a trusted residential, commercial & agricultural solar installer in Tamil Nadu.

2028 – 2030

Solar EPC Expansion

Scaling into large industrial and government EPC projects across South India.

2030 – 2033

Setting up our first solar panel assembly plant.

2033 – 2036

Solar Module Manufacturing

2036 – 2040

Solar Cell Manufacturing

Backward integration into solar cell production.

2040+

Establishing Silisun as a recognized international clean energy brand.
