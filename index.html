Panel Technology

Solar Panel Types We Supply & Install

We source from trusted manufacturers and recommend the right panel technology based on your roof, budget, and energy goals.

◆

Monocrystalline Panels

Highest efficiency (20–23%). Best performance in limited space. Ideal for residential rooftops.

Efficiency: 20–23%
◇

Polycrystalline Panels

Good efficiency at lower cost. Slightly larger footprint. Excellent for larger rooftops and open land.

Efficiency: 15–18%
▭

Bifacial Panels

Generate power from both front and rear surfaces. Higher yield — ideal for ground-mounted systems and flat rooftops.

Efficiency: 21–25%
▬

Thin-Film Panels

Flexible and lightweight. Suitable for curved surfaces, carports, and large industrial shed rooftops.

Efficiency: 11–14%
